Our Mission

Predict to Succeed Better

ScorePredict was designed to help students anticipate their academic performance based on their past results and other relevant factors. Our prediction model uses advanced machine learning algorithms to provide accurate estimates.

How Does It Work?

Our system analyzes three key factors:

  • Competition ID - Each competition has its own characteristics and difficulty levels.
  • Baccalaureate Average - Your performance in the baccalaureate is an important indicator of your overall academic level.
  • Previous Results - Your previous performance in similar competitions.

This data is processed by our model, which generates a score prediction based on the trends and patterns identified in our extensive training dataset.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the accuracy of the prediction model?

Our model has been trained on thousands of historical data points and achieves an average accuracy of 85%. However, predictions should be considered as indicators and not as guaranteed results.

Are my data secure?

Absolutely. We do not store any personal data on our servers. All saved predictions are stored solely on your device (in your browser's localStorage).

How can I improve my predicted score?

The recommendations provided after each prediction are personalized based on your score. Following these tips can help improve your performance. Generally, strengthening your knowledge in fundamental subjects and practicing regularly with similar exams are good strategies.
